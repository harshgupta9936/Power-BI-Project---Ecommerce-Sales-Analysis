📌 Project Title: E-Commerce Sales Intelligence Dashboard (Power BI)



📌 Description :

* Developed an end-to-end Power BI dashboard analyzing 1M+ transactional records across multiple platforms (Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, etc.)
* Designed interactive dashboards covering sales, product performance, customer behavior, platform analysis, and profit-discount relationships
* Built advanced DAX measures including AOV, YoY Growth, Profit Margin, Running Totals, and Moving Averages
* Implemented time-series forecasting and trend analysis for business planning
* Identified key insights such as 50%+ revenue dependency on Electronics, stable demand patterns, and weak correlation between ratings and sales
* Performed customer segmentation analysis (age groups, spending behavior) to uncover targeting opportunities
* Analyzed discount vs profit impact, revealing inefficiencies in high-discount clusters
* Designed a Strategic Insights dashboard translating data into actionable business recommendations
* Improved decision-making by highlighting risk areas, growth opportunities, and optimization strategies



📌 Tech Stack:

* Power BI
* DAX
* Data Modeling
* Excel / CSV
