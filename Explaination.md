1\. Problem Statement



“I worked on an e-commerce dataset with over 1 million records. The goal was to analyze business performance and build a dashboard that not only shows metrics but also provides actionable insights for decision-making.”





2\. Approach



“I structured the project into multiple analytical layers:



* Executive overview for KPIs
* Sales intelligence for trends and forecasting
* Product and customer analysis for deeper insights
* Platform and discount analysis for profitability
* And finally a strategic insights page for business decisions”





3\. Technical Work



“I used Power BI with DAX to create measures like:



* Total Sales, Profit, AOV
* Year-over-Year Growth
* Running totals and moving averages
* Profit Margin and discount impact analysis



I also performed time-series forecasting and built interactive filters for dynamic analysis.”





4\. Key Insights



“Some important insights I found were:



* Electronics contributed over 50% of revenue
* Sales were highly stable month-to-month
* Customer segments had similar spending patterns
* Ratings had very little impact on sales
* Discounts were consistent but not always efficient”





5\. Business Impact



“From these insights, I identified risks like category dependency and lack of segmentation, and suggested strategies such as:



* Diversifying product categories
* Optimizing discount strategies
* Targeting high-value customer segments
* Focusing on high-margin products”





6\. Closing (IMPORTANT)



“This project helped me understand how to convert raw data into business insights and recommendations, which is critical for real-world decision-making.”
