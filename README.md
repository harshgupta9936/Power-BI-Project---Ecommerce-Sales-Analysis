📊 E-Commerce Sales Intelligence Dashboard


🚀 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Power BI dashboard built on a large-scale e-commerce dataset (\~1 million records).
It focuses on transforming raw transactional data into actionable business insights for decision-making.


📁 Dataset Features

* Order ID, Product, Category
* Price, Quantity, Total Sales
* Profit, Discount %
* Customer Segment, Age
* Region, Platform
* Order Date (2024–2026)


📌 Key Objectives

* Analyze sales and profit performance
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Understand customer behavior and segmentation
* Evaluate platform performance
* Study discount impact on profitability
* Provide strategic business recommendations

📈 Key Insights

* Electronics contributes 50%+ of total revenue
* Sales show stable monthly patterns (\~₹3.3–3.5B)
* Customer segments have uniform spending (\~₹39K)
* Weak correlation between ratings and sales
* Discounts (\~17–18%) have limited impact on margins


⚠️ Business Risks

* Over-dependence on Electronics category
* Increasing discount trends affecting profitability
* Lack of platform differentiation
* No strong customer segmentation strategy


🚀 Recommendations

* Diversify product portfolio
* Optimize discount strategies
* Focus on high-margin products
* Implement targeted marketing campaigns
* Develop platform-specific strategies
